About Sam Kissner

Sam is not only a great brother and friend, but is also a musician, writer, martial artist and software developer. He was born on December 28, 1989 along with his brother, Daniel Kissner, who is currently designing this page. Sam graduated from the University of Nebraska Omaha with a bachelor's degree in Chemistry and a Master's degree in Computer Science. He lives with his girlfriend and their cat.
